Scratch Game: Spell Slinger

Game Link

Description

My game consists of a pokemon battle based game with a magic/fantasy esque theme.
My scoring system was based upon the same as pokemon, with the addition of a "Combo Charge",
a special condition that allows for more spells to be created. The randomness occurs during the
enemies' attacks. The game gets difficult as the game progesses and you continue to battle enemies.
The game finally ends when you defeat all enemies or lose if you health falls to 0.

Reflection

I made this game because I really like pokemon's system and I wanted to take a magical take on
it while making it RPG based. My two successful points are setting all my sprites and setting the
battle system such as making the health fall and so on. Two obstacles I came across was the complexity
of the system and the amount of effort and time needed to fix things and set an idea. It took a while for
me to figure things out and I slowly ran out of time and couldn't finish my project. If I had more time,
I would've completely finished my game and made it look smoother.

I chose this algorithm as it is the basis of my battle system. It allows me to set rules for how the hp falls.
